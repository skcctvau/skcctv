About SKCCTV: Protecting Queensland for Over 4 Years

Our Journey: A Commitment to Community Safety

SKCCTV, or Singh and Kaur CCTV, was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide the local communities of Brisbane, the Sunshine Coast, and the Gold Coast with reliable, high-quality, and affordable security solutions. Our journey began over four years ago, and in that time, we've grown from a small operation to a trusted name in the security industry.

As a family-owned business, we understand the importance of feeling safe in your home and workplace. We treat every client's property as our own, ensuring meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to excellence that is second to none. Our reputation is built on trust, honesty, and a passion for what we do.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the leading provider of security and surveillance systems in Queensland, empowering our clients with the tools and technology to protect their most valuable assets. We achieve this by offering:

  • The highest quality products from global leaders like Dahua and Hikvision.
  • Professional and seamless installation by our licensed and certified teams.
  • Exceptional customer service and ongoing support.
  • Tailored solutions that perfectly match our clients' unique security needs.

Our Expertise & Credentials

Licensed & Insured

We are a fully licensed and insured security firm. Our team holds all necessary security licenses to install, maintain, and service security systems throughout Queensland.

Industry Professionals

Our technicians are highly trained, experienced, and stay up-to-date with the latest security technology to provide you with the best possible service.
